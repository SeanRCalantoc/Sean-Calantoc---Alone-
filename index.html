Alone?

You wake up in a grocery store and have no memory of how you got there. The air is humid, and there is a buzzing noise that fills the store

Amongst the buzzing, you hear faint wimpering from across the aisle


The moment you let a word out of your mouth, all the lights shut off...except for the one in front of you. There stands a silhoette.

Looks like you're not alone anymore :)



You walk out of the aisle as the wimpering continues and see a door

What now?



You enter into an empty hallway. Suddenly, the door locks, and you can't go back. There's no exit for as long as your eye can see, and you lose all hope.

Well, at least you're still alone, right?
